## Information We Collect

When you install and use SenSpy, we may collect the following types of information:

- **Device information:** Device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.  
- **Network information:** Wi-Fi network name (SSID), IP address, and local network identifiers required for detecting devices.  
- **Sensor information:** Magnetic field values, infrared light detection (via camera), and Wi-Fi scan data used to perform hidden device detection.  
- **Usage data:** Interactions within the app, such as scans performed and results generated.  
- **Optional information:** If you choose to contact us directly (e.g., email support), we may store your email address and any information you provide in your message.  

We do **not** collect personally identifiable information such as name, gender, or contacts.

## How We Use Information

We use the information we collect solely for the following purposes:

- To provide the core functionality of SenSpy (Wi-Fi scans, infrared detection, magnetic detection).  
- To display relevant information to the user, such as detected devices, IP addresses, and scan results.  
- To improve performance and troubleshoot errors.  
- To ensure the safety and integrity of the Service.  

We do **not** sell or rent your information to third parties.

4. Select **SenSpy** from your active subscriptions.
